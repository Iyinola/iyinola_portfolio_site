About me

Iyinola is a software developer who has designed websites for major corporations in Nigeria, Africa and Abroad.

With her early background in Finance, she is also passionate about financial technology and strives to use her skill for the growth of FinTech in Africa.

Iyinola is currently a Product Manager at Sterling Bank where she uses her creative mind to solve real life problems.
